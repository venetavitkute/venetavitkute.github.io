About Me

Hello everyone! I am Veneta Vitkute, a professional Makeup Artist based in Lithuania, Klaipeda. I have been a makeup artist for more than 7 years. I started my journey working in one of the most famous beauty salons “Alina Guess Makeup Academy.” I had the opportunity to travel to London, Ireland and Norway with the owner of this famous salon to give make-up lessons to other girls. I have done more than 100 make-up jobs for women and I only work with professional cosmetics brands such as Chanel, Dior, MAC, Sla Paris, Estee Lauder, Giorgio Armani, and Laura Mercier. I approach my work with great responsibility and strive for continuous improvement in my profession. I don't look at make-up as a means to hide your flaws, but rather to enhance your beauty and make you feel like a real queen!
